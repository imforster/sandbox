Fullscreen on iPhone

iPhone Safari can't make a web page fullscreen directly. To play chrome-free in landscape:

  1. Tap the Safari Share button (the square with an up arrow)
  2. Choose Add to Home Screen, then Add
  3. Open the game from the new home-screen icon — it launches full screen
  4. Rotate to landscape and tap START
